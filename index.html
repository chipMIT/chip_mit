Introducing ChipMIT
ChipMIT is a student-driven initiative focused on microelectronics, digital systems, and VLSI design. It’s a place for hands-on learning, innovation, and collaboration, empowering future tech leaders to explore the world of chip design and build impactful solutions.
Our Vision & Mission
Vision
To cultivate a thriving community of VLSI enthusiasts, empowering them with technical knowledge and industry relevant skills.
Mission
To provide applied learning, foster innovative projects, and create a collaborative platform for budding VLSI enthusiasts in campus.
Our Domains & Team
We have many domains relevant to chip design, crafted with respect to abstraction.
Technical Domains

Analog Design

Explores the principles of real-world electronic circuits like amplifiers and filters.

Digital Design

Focuses on understanding digital logic and designing circuits for practical applications.

Computer Architecture

Explains the basics of processor design and system architecture with hands-on tasks.

Simulation & Design division

Covers hardware description and automation techniques for electronic systems.
